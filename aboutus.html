About Us

Welcome to Hotel DreamStay — A World of Timeless Luxury

At Hotel DreamStay, luxury is not just an experience; it is a way of life. Ideally located near the sacred Mahalaxmi Mandir in Kolhapur, our hotel offers an oasis of elegance, comfort, and personalized service for discerning travelers from around the world.

Every element at Hotel Kiyara has been carefully designed to inspire and enchant. Our luxurious rooms and suites seamlessly blend contemporary sophistication with timeless charm, offering bespoke furnishings, world-class amenities, and serene views that celebrate the spirit of Kolhapur.

Indulge your senses at our signature restaurant, relax and rejuvenate at the Kiyara Spa, or savor handcrafted cocktails with breathtaking rooftop views. Whether you're visiting for a spiritual journey, a grand celebration, or a private retreat, every moment at Hotel Kiyara is crafted to be unforgettable.

Our dedicated team is passionate about delivering intuitive, discreet service, ensuring that every guest feels not only welcomed but truly cared for.

Hotel Kiyara — where devotion meets luxury, and every stay becomes a cherished memory.
